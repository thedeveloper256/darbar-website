Our Story

Rooted in tradition,
crafted for today

Darba was born from a simple belief — that great food is an act of care. We draw inspiration from East Africa's rich culinary heritage, pairing time-honoured recipes with the finest seasonal ingredients sourced from local farmers and producers.

Every dish that leaves our kitchen carries with it the intention of the hands that shaped it: honest, generous, and full of character.
