Our Story

Rooted in tradition,
crafted for today

Darbar was born from a simple belief — that great food is an act of care. We draw inspiration from East Africa's rich culinary heritage, pairing time-honoured recipes with the finest seasonal ingredients sourced from local farmers and producers.

Every dish that leaves our kitchen carries with it the intention of the hands that shaped it: honest, generous, and full of character.

The Experience

Evening Dining

As the sun dips below Kampala's skyline, Darbar transforms into an intimate sanctuary. Candlelit tables, curated music, and attentive service that never intrudes — only enhances. The perfect setting for a celebration or a quiet evening for two.

🌿

Outdoor Terrace

Dine beneath the open Kampala sky on our lush garden terrace. Surrounded by greenery and the gentle hum of the city, it's the ideal spot for leisurely lunches and sunset gatherings with family and friends.

🎉

Private Events

From birthday celebrations to corporate dinners, our private dining space accommodates up to 40 guests. Custom menus, dedicated service, and an ambiance tailored to your occasion — every detail considered.
